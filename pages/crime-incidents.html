--- title: Crime Incidents 2006 - Present description: |

Philadelphia Police Department crime incidents from calendar year 2006 to present, including Part I and Part II crimes. UCR codes are generalized and incident locations are rounded to the hundred block. These counts may not coincide exactly with data that is submitted to the Uniformed Crime Reporting (UCR) system.

Note: All figures presented are based on preliminary Philadelphia Police Department crime data. This crime data is subject to reclassification upon further investigation.
