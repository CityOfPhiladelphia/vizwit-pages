--- title: City Payments description: |

This dataset includes checks and ACH payments made by the City during the fiscal year, which runs from July 1st through June 31st. This dataset does not include all payments made by the City. Namely, salary and benefits payments are not included here, but information on City salaries can be found in the employee salaries and overtime dataset.

Please note: bar chart data is pulled from the city_payments_aggregates dataset. Filter table data is pulled from the city_payments dataset. For more details, see the metadata.
